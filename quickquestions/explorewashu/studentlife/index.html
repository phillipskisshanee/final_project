ACRONYMS

  • Let's make this quick!

    • DUC = Danforth University Center
    BD = Bear's Den, dining place on the 40
    RA = Residental Advisor
    WUSA = Washington University Student Associates, a student who helps with the college transition
    The 40 = The 40 arces of land that underclassmen live on
    W.I.L.D. = Walk in Lay Down, a free concert on campus

POPULAR TOPICS ON CAMPUS

  • So who is Connie?

    • We are glad you asked! Connie Diekman, is the director of university nutrition at Wash U. So if your favorite fattening food around campus just magically disappears *cough, mozz sticks, cough*, then Connie is probably responsibe for it.

    Connie's Choice is a program that let's students know some of the healthiest options on campus. If Connie approves, then we approve



  • Why is the Bunny Statue wearing a sweater?

    • A few years ago, one brave student decided it was her duty to knit clothing for the Bunny. Throughout the winter season, the bunny can be seen wearing a varitey of scarves and sweaters. Dressing the Bunny has even become so popular that big events like Relay for Life or Thurtene Carnival will use the statue to advertise

TELL ME THE FREE STUFF!

  • Did someone say free food?

    • Just about every student group may try to bribe potential new members by offering free food at their general body and interest meetings. But there are also formal Wash U events that offer this too. Happy Hour is a cool free event that happens almost every Thursday at 5 PM in the Gargoyle. At Happy Hour, students can indulge in free pizza, sandwitches and soda. And if you are 21, there is free beer too!
