COMMUNITY SERVICE

  • How can I get involved in community service?

    • The Gephardt Institute for Civic and Community Engagement is an awesome resourse for community serivce. Check out their website!



  • I heard about the Campus Y, what is that?

    • The Campus Y is an on campus chapter of the YMCA. This organization offers many great programs ranging for tutoring programs to leadership and mentoring positions.

    The Campus Y is located on the bottom floor of Umrath Hall. Stop by and say hi to Kathy and Jess!
