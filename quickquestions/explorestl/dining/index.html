LOCAL FAVORITES

  • Ted Drewes

    • Ted Drewes is a local frozen custard business that offers many great frozen treats. Founded by Ted Drewes Sr. and continued by Ted Drewes Jr., Ted Drewes Frozen Custard is a food destination that should be at the top of your list.



  • Imo's Pizza

    • Imo's Pizza is a local food chain whose pizza has a signature square shape.

WASH U FAVORITES

  • The Delmar Loop

    • The Loop offers many popular Wash U dining locations. Interested in Chinese food? Try Corner 17. Want to eat a good burger? 3 Kings is your place.

    A personal Quick Questions favorite is Mission Taco. This beautiful place offers $2 tacos every night after 10 PM. And for you of-age drinkers out there, they have $2 shots too.



  • Clayton

    • If you love brunch then you have to travel to the Clayton area. Located only a couple of minutes from the Danforth Campus, the Clayton area gives foodies meals to rave about.

    Half & Half has many egg dishes that are sure to make your tummy happy. Another Wash U favorite is Winslow's Home. This location is small, but don't let that long wait stop you from going. Winslow's is definitely worth the trip.

SPECIAL EVENTS

  • Food Truck Fridays

    • Food Truck Friday is an event where resturants who obivously havea food truck, come together in a local park in St. Louis to sell their delicious cuisine to everyone. The exact location of Food Truck Fridays changes all the time so make sure you follow their Facebook page so you will always know where to find them.



  • Food Festivals

    • Throughout the year, many areas in St. Louis will have food festivals that typically have cultural influences. While majority of these events are free to attend, you will need to purchase a ticket to ensure that you can eat all the food at the venue.
