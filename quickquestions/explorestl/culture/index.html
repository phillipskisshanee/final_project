CULTURE

  • Is everyone in St. Louis a Cardinals fan?

    • While everyone in the city may not be a fan, it is very obvious that the Cardinals are loved by many in the area. With the team having great games throughout the season, there is always something to talk about. The St. Louis cardinals even went to the 2011 World Series Championships which generated even more love for them in the city.

    So whether you are a baseball fan or not, when the Cardinals are playing at home you are likely to hear about it.



  • What is like to live in St. Louis?

    • It would be hard for us to explain all of the awesome things about being a St. Louis resident, so we won't explain it. Instead you can take a look at the city's offical website to read up about the things that make this city great.

    We would just add that St. Louis is a vibrant city with many cool, laid-back people that you should to get to know.
